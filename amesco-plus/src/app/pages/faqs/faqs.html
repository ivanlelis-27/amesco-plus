FAQ
Can I earn points in Amesco Plus and use the points to purchase Amesco products?
Yes. You may use your points to purchase products from Amesco stores.
How do I become a member of Amesco Plus?
Download Amesco Plus app from your phone.
How do I earn points?
Purchase at least P200 to earn one (1) point.
What is the point equivalent in Philippine Peso?
One (1) point is equivalent to P1.00 if cash transaction; (One) 1 point = P0.75 (credit card transaction)
Is there a required minimum purchase to earn points?
NONE
Is there a required minimum points for redemption?
NONE
Can I redeem my points from any Amesco Drug outlet?
Yes, points are redeemable from any Amesco Drug outlet.
Can I convert my points to cash?
No, points may only be used for purchasing Amesco products from Amesco stores.
